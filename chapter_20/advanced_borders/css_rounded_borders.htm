Rounding the edges

This is a div element with uniform and equal borders across all edges.
This div element's round edges are specified with two arguments. Top left and bottom right edges are equal, just like top right and bottom left corners are equal as well.
This div element's round edges are specified with three arguments. Top right and bottom left coreners are equal.
This div element's round edges are specified with four arguments.
This div element's round edges are specified individually through the four properties that define round borders (border-top-left-radius, border-top-right-radius, border-bottom-left-radius & border-bottom-right-radius). As you can see, the same effect as with the rounding with four arguments can be achieved, since the latter is just a short version of the former.
This div element has ellipse-shaped borders, since the / operator is used when specifying its borders' rounding.
